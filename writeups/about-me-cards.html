About Me

1. Change your profile picture on Canvas to the picture of your face.

2. You will be provided  with an index card. With this card, please follow the following instructions:

     Attach a picture of yourself to the front of your index card (See sample below)and write your full name near your picture. Your picture must be a current picture of your face  (not your cutest baby picture, not in your Spiderman suit, not in your Pikachu costume.). I need the photos to help me learn your names, so it must look like you look now.

     Under your name, write you name phonetically to help me learn the correct pronunciation of your name.

     Then on the other side and answer the following questions. They can be hand written. You do not have to copy the questions down word by word, but a couple of words to tell me which question you are answering is helpful. See the example I provided.

    1. What is your major?

    2. What are your educational goals? Transfer to UC? USC? CalState?  Get a job as a . . . ?

    3. What CS classes have you taken? Where? (PCC? GCC? etc.)

    4. What was the last math class you took? How long ago did you take it?

    5. What classes are you taking this semester?

    6. Do you plan to have a job this semester? What do you do and how many hours do you work?

    7. What are some of your hobbies? (besides Computer / Video games)

    8. Share something interesting about yourself.
