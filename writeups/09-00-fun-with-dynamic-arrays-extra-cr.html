Extra Credit: Pointers

Explore pointers by writing a complete C++ program that demonstrates a variety of pointer operations.

Requirements

  • Declare pointers of different types.
  • Assign pointers using addresses.
  • Dereference pointers to read/write values.
  • Pass pointers to functions.
  • Return pointers where appropriate.
  • Demonstrate pointer arithmetic or pointers with arrays (if covered).
  • Include clear labeled output and comments explaining each demonstration.

General Expectations

  • Organize your solution into multiple functions.
  • Compile without warnings or errors.
  • Produce clear, labeled output.
  • Include comments explaining what each section demonstrates.
  • The goal is to explore the topic and demonstrate understanding, not to build a large application.
