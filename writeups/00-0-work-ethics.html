WORK ETHICS:

  • Take pride in your work
  • Start early. Stay late.  work hard.
  • Have good style: Indent, comment, test.
    • Test, Test, Test!
    • Test every conceivable scenario.
      • Edge cases.
      • Empty cases
      • Too much data cases
  • Programs you write are very often tools you will use over and over again.
    • Be kind to your future self
    • watch out for bugs. They may look harmless now, but later, they will grow fangs and claws!
  • Projects overlap:
    • We will discuss a project while you are deeply involved in another project
      • Do not tune out. Pay attention to what's going on in class.
      • Do not think "I will ask someone after class." Be the one others ask for help.
    • Projects are related to each other. You will learn something in one project that you will use in another.
  • Projects might seem daunting
    • They are not as scary once you start.
    • Do NOT drag your feet. I will HURT YOU if you do. 
      • By this, I mean: "Nobody else has started / completed the project, so, maybe we'll get an extension. 
      • It lowers the standard of the class and I will not allow it.
  • Working together:
    • You can work together. But when your work starts to look similar, you have gone too far.
    • Do NOT look at someone else's code.
    • Do NOT let someone else to see your code.
    • Do not copy code from the web.
    • Do your own work. It's hard, but this is what you need to learn the material.
    • It gets easier.
  • The 4.0 club:
    • There are two types of students with very high GPA:
      • Those who get an A at any cost
      • and those who get an A at any cost.
      • DO NOT BE IN THE FIRST GROUP!!
  • READ THE DAMN BOOK!
    • do all the self test exercises.
  • BE ONTIME and stay for the entire duration of the class
    • When you are late,  you are telling me you do not care about your own education.
    • You disrupt the flow of the class
    • It's disrespectful to the class, your colleagues, and to me.
  • Get off your F$#&@ng phone!
    • It's rude!
    • call me out if I do it.
    • Give the class your full attention.
  • When I stop picking on you, that's when you should start to worry!!
